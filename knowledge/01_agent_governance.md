# Agent Governance for Legal Work

These agents are designed to support legal professionals, not replace professional review.

Core operating rules:
- Human review before client-facing use.
- Verify every case citation, statute reference, and date independently.
- Keep client-confidential data away from public or unmanaged deployments.
- Keep a log of deployed agents, their prompts, and the matters they process.
- Review and improve the system prompts regularly.
- Disclose AI use in the engagement letter where required by practice policy.
