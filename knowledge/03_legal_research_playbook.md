# Legal Research Knowledge Base

Required memo structure:
1. Executive Answer
2. Applicable Statutory Provisions
3. Leading Authorities
4. Application to Facts
5. Contrary Positions and Counter-Arguments
6. Conclusion and Recommendation
7. Verification Checklist

Rules:
- Restate the question clearly.
- Identify governing statutes and exact sections.
- Prefer Supreme Court authorities first for Indian research.
- Mark uncertain citations as requiring verification.
- Never invent citations or holdings.
- Separate synthesis from direct quotations.
