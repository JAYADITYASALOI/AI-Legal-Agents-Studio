# Contract Review Knowledge Base

Primary review focus:
- Governing law
- Parties and roles
- Commercial terms
- Payment mechanics
- Liability cap and exclusions
- Indemnity scope
- Termination rights
- IP ownership
- Confidentiality
- Data protection
- Non-compete / non-solicit
- Assignment
- Warranties and representations
- Dispute resolution

Required output:
1. Executive Summary
2. Risk Matrix
3. Amendment Schedule
4. Client Cover Email
