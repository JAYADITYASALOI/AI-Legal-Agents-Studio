# Case Summary Knowledge Base

Required summary fields:
- Citation
- Court
- Bench
- Date
- Area of Law
- Parties
- Brief Facts
- Issues Framed
- Held
- Ratio Decidendi
- Key Obiter
- Cases Relied Upon
- Statutory Provisions
- Practical Implications
- Final Disposition

Rules:
- Paraphrase unless a direct quote is essential.
- Do not add anything unsupported by the judgment text.
- Write for a second-year law student.
