# Compliance Alert Knowledge Base

Typical areas:
- DPDP Act and rules
- SEBI circulars
- RBI circulars
- MCA notifications
- Companies Act updates

The alert should:
- Distinguish substantive changes from procedural or administrative changes.
- Summarise the update clearly.
- Identify affected practice areas.
- State likely client impact.
- Recommend the next action.
- Provide a concise forwardable email.
